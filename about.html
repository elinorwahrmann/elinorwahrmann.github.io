Meet Elinor Wahrmann, Therapist in Los Altos, California

You deserve to be supported by someone who gets it.

My Style

In our work together, you are the priority.

I bring warmth, compassion, and a curious mindset with a hint of humor to our sessions.

As a mother of three who relocated to the Bay area, I understand the daily struggle to be everything to everyone while trying to navigate the challenges of adjusting to a new life, and make meaning of my personal journey.

In our work together, we will tap into your inner strength and develop practical strategies to empower you to take control of your life and move towards a more fulfilling and satisfying future.

Together, we can create a roadmap towards the life you truly want.
